1. Data Collection

Nomodose is designed with a strong focus on user privacy. The Application does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Account or registration information
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history or network activity
  • Location data

No user data is sent to external servers.

2. On-Device Data (Local Only)

Nomodose stores app data locally on your device to provide core functionality, such as:

  • Usage logs (counts and timestamps)
  • Plan settings (baseline, quit date, schedule parameters)
  • Preferences (reminders, tracking mode)

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with the Service Provider or third parties.

3. Data Sharing

The Application does not share any data with third parties. We do not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

4. Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected and no data is stored on external servers, all app data remains on your device. You can remove all app data at any time by using in-app delete options (if available) or uninstalling the Application.

5. User Rights

Because we do not collect or process personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete via a request. You remain in control of your local data on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Security

Since the Application does not collect or transmit user data, there is no risk of personal data being intercepted or stored externally. All functionality operates locally on your device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the Application indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Consent

By using the Application, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact: support@nomodose.app
